Here's a little bit about me...

Hello! My name is Jared, I grew up in a small suburb called Lyncourt just on the outskirts of Syracuse, New York. Growing up I've always managed to find a perfect balance between nature and technology, being close to both the city and more rural areas helped a lot.

I enjoy staying physically active and keeping my mind strong by going to the gym for at least 1 hour, 5 times a week. To keep my mind healthy I practice meditation and read a lot of philosophy, from people such as Marcus Aurelius and Miyamoto Mushashi

I think what led me to pursue software development was just seeing the world change so rapidly as I've grown up. It seems like just yesterday to me that the first IPhone was released, how far we've come since just then alone baffles me. After a long period of questioning myself I decided I no longer wanted to be a bystander in this amazing world of technology. I want to be part of the change that I've seen, I fully believe I'm capable of amazing things!

As far as my goals go I am a very ambitious person, I would like to secure a position as a Full Stack FAANG Software Engineer. I would love to stay specifically with Amazon as I think it would be absolutely amazing to go from a T1 employee to such a high position. I also find the Amazon work culture very welcoming and think that I would fit in very well, however I don't want to stop there. I eventually would like to lead a team of developers as I have the natural ability to be a leader.
